Jakes Perfect Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Pickles (from Prismo)
  • Dill
  • Diced boiled eggs
  • Bird (from the window)
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Sliced Roma tomatoes
  • Sweet yellow onions
  • Tears (for salt)
  • Meat prepared sous-vide with rosemary and thyme (possibly a play on words from "Time Sandwich")
  • Bacon
  • A lobster's soul

Recipe instructions

  1. [sharpening knives] One, two, three, four, five, six. [putting herbs on a steak]
  2. Rosemary, thyme. [puts the steak in a bag, seals it, and places it in a pot of hot water]
  3. Sous-vide. Keep that at 135 degrees. [cuts a loaf of bread in half and uses a blowtorch to toast the inside]
  4. All right, now we're gettin' somewhere. [places each ingredient on the bread as he says their names]
  5. Cream cheese! Pickles from my boy Prismo! And some dill! [grabs a bird from the windowsill]
  6. Diced boiled eggs! Bird from the window! Yeah, baby, now we're rollin'! Whoo!
  7. Common cucumber! Sliced Roma tomato. Sweet yellow onion—organic. [tears up]
  8. Oh, almost done. [wipes tears away and puts them in the sandwich]
  9. Tears for salt. Meat prepared sous-vide. ♪Bacon.♪ [holds up a lobster]
  10. You're the most important part. [He puts the lobster in a pot of boiling water,
  11. and its soul escapes. Jake shoos it onto the sandwich.] Lobster soul. [puts on the top piece of bread,
  12. completing the sandwich and causing it to glow blue] It's amazing! Ohhh,
  13. [falls backwards, and Jake and BMO catch him] this is the greatest sandwich I've ever made!
